Connecting the dots: Mapping of farms producers and consumers in madurai

TEAM DESCRIPTION

Bon Vivant – a sociable person who has cultivated and refined tastes, especially with respect to food and drink.

We have different cuisines and different kinds of food like pre-cooked, frozen, canned, etc… and different types of meals like starters, entrée, and desserts. No matter the advancement in the food industry, it all starts with the farmer. Farmer has been and always will be the ruler of the vast food industry kingdom. We the bon vivant engineers are here to stand in the gap to map the ruler (the farmer) with the subjects (the stakeholders) in a profitable way.

Unleashing Food Area 51

Overproduction is a common problem among farmers but not among the general public. Our main aim is to provide maps of the location of farmlands, hotels, catering services, food processing industries, hostels, orphanages, and temples in Madurai. With this GIS-based information system-assisted data analysis, a network of various agricultural sectors can be connected directly without any middleman in order to reduce wastage and promote the farmer’s well-being thereby benefitting farmers and the community in every aspect.

Motivation

As Shakespeare said “All the world’s a stage”, and the stage for the Bon Vivant Engineers is the Mapathon. With great privilege and honour, we take Mapathon as an opportunity to exhibit our GIS-based maps which connect the farmers and the stakeholders in a more profitable way for the farmers.
